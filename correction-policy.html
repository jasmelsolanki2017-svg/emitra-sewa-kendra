Correction Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

We try to keep E-MITRA WALA information accurate and updated. If any page has an error, outdated date, broken link or unclear detail, users can request a correction.

How to Request a Correction

  • Email the page URL, issue details and correct source to support@emitrawala.online.
  • For job, result or admit card information, include the official notification or official website link where possible.
  • We review correction requests and update pages when the correction is verified.

Important Note

Recruitment dates, vacancies, eligibility and fees can change by official department update. Users should always verify details from official sources before applying.
